Homemade Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 medium-sized apples (peeled, cored , and sliced)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter (cut into small pieces)
  • 2 unbaked pie crusts (homemade or store-bought)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220℃)
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar ,flour ,cinnamon and salt. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.
  3. Place one of the pie crusts into 9-inch pie dish.
  4. Pour the apple mixture into the pic crust,spreading it out evenly.
  5. Dot the top of the apple mixture with small pieces of butter.
  6. Place the second pie crust over the apples and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  7. Using a shark knife, make a few small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  8. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 45-55 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  9. Remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool for at least 30 minutes before serving

Serving Suggestion:

Serve you homemade apple pie warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delicious treat!

Enjoy!
